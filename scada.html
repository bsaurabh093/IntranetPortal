SCADA

  • Overview
  • Thrust Areas of BD

The SCADA system provides the real time Monitoring and Control of important parameters of gas pipelines and associated facilities for safe, reliable and efficient operation of gas pipelines.The SCADA system has two parts: Data Acquisition & Supervisory Control.

The Data acquisition system collects data from field instruments installed along the pipeline at various locations, processes the data and presents the data to operator.

Supervisory control provides additional facility of controlling the field devices like valves, machines, flow controllers, pressure controllers, etc. by remote operations.

SCADA system provides facility for remote monitoring and controlling of pipelines from National Gas Management Center, Noida, various Regional Gas Management Center and O&M control rooms of respective pipelines across GAIL
